WHY DIVE WITH US?

Every day we schedule 2 morning boat dives (at 9.00 and 11.00), 2 in the afternoon (at 1.00 and 2.30), and night dives, when requested. We ask that you arrive at the shop 30 minutes beforehand to arrange paperwork and equipment as necessary, as well as attend a quick briefing. All of our dives are guided by a PADI professional who knows the sites and where to find the good stuff! If you would like to do a number of dives with us, why not purchase a package? The larger the package, the less you pay per dive. Our packages range from 4 dives to 10, and can include excursions like our trip to Du Du Caverns. You can do up to 4 dives a day, however you will probably choose to spread out your package over a few days or a week!

Environment

The average water temperature of Sosua Bay is 25-29C / 76-82F; our divers usually wear shorties though many choose to go without! We are also blessed with beautiful visibility, averaging 15-30 m (50-100 ft.) depending on environmental conditions. And for those who are not staying in Sosua, dont worry! We are close to Cabarete, not too far from Amber Cove cruise port, Maimon, Cofresi, Costambar and Puerto Plata.
